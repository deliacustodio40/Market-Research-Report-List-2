<p><p>The Head Motion Capture System market consists of three types: Magnetic Motion Capture, Mechanical Motion Capture, and Optical Motion Capture. Magnetic Motion Capture uses magnetic fields to track the movement of the head, typically through sensors attached to the headgear. Mechanical Motion Capture involves attaching physical sensors to the head, which detect movement and transfer it to a computer system. Optical Motion Capture uses cameras and markers to track the position and orientation of the head. These systems are utilized in various applications such as animation, gaming, sports analysis, and virtual reality.</p></p>
