<p><p>In the 18650 Battery Pack market, there are two types: series battery pack and parallel battery pack. A series battery pack connects the batteries in a linear manner, maximizing voltage while keeping the capacity unchanged. This is suitable for applications requiring higher voltage. On the other hand, a parallel battery pack connects the batteries side by side, maximizing capacity while maintaining voltage. This type is preferable for applications needing longer runtime. Both market types offer different benefits and are used in various industries depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
