Lastly, the "others" category includes alternative anode materials, such as silicon nanowires or silicon-based alloys, which are being explored for their potential in lithium-silicon batteries. These materials aim to address the limitations of traditional graphite anodes and improve battery capacity and lifespan.</p></p>
