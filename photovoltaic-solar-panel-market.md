Mono panels are made from a single high-grade silicone crystal, offering higher efficiency and better performance in low light conditions. Polycrystalline panels are made from multiple silicone crystals, providing lower efficiency but at a more affordable cost. Thin film panels use a different manufacturing process, resulting in lower efficiency but greater flexibility and versatility for installation. These different types cater to varying consumer needs and preferences in the solar energy market.</p></p>
