<p><p>Moka pots, also known as stovetop espresso makers, are traditional coffee brewing devices that originated in Italy. These pots consist of a bottom chamber filled with water, a middle funnel-shaped filter basket that holds the ground coffee, and a top chamber where the brewed coffee collects. When heated on a stovetop or a heat source, the water in the bottom chamber gets pressurized, forcing it through the coffee grounds and into the top chamber, producing a rich, full-bodied coffee flavor.</p><p>The future outlook for the Moka Pots Market appears to be positive.
